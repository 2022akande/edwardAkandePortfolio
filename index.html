About Me

Hello, my name is Edward Akande and I am a Junior Web Developer.
I started programming around the age of 11, I would develop web
pages comprised exclusively of HTML. As I progressed I learned to
incorporate powerful technologies like React and Bootstrap. My most
recent work includes personal projects and a few commissioned
sites all of which can be found under the work section. My philosophy
when it comes to web development can be summarized by the phrase "why not"
I want to be a part of the wave of web developers who are pushing the
envelope and erasing the limitations of what a website can be. It
always brings me joy to see the full feature in-browser experiences like
Kapwing and Pixlr. Those developers say why not to extensive photo
editing right in the browser, They say why not to editing entire videos
from a website. I believe we will see a day where the only question
standing between us and the web design projects of the future is simply: why not?

Q: Do you prefer to work alone or on a team?

A: A team. There are many pros to working on a team the most obvious being a lighter workload.
In addition to less work, you're also exposed to other peoples code, meaning you might pick up some
of their good habits. Every developer out there has something to teach me whether they've been coding
8 years or 8 months.


Q: What web browser do you use?

A: All of them. As a web developer, I have to see how my code interacts with any web browser
a user might find themselves on.
